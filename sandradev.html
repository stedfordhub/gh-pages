Here's a little bit about me

I’ve made my home base in Chicago Illinois. My journey into tech started with a degree in economics. As I started sharing my writing online, I was fascinated by all the beautiful designs and graphics and how easily I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. I believe images and graphics are at the core of building functional and attractive web properties. How one crafts and positions those images is critical to becoming a great story teller.

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
